Why Coral Reefs are important?

Coral reefs may make up only 0.01% of the ocean but also have 25% of all marine life in the ocean! This means if coral reefs are destroyed a lot of marine life will also perish!

Why is this happening?

Well, coral reefs mainly die from something called Coral Bleaching. This is when the coral are exposed to hot temperatures or in hot waters, causing the coral to become a white coral (Coral Bleaching)

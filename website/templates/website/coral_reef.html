Coral reefs may make up only 0.01% of the ocean but also have 25% of all marine life in the ocean! This means if coral reefs are destroyed a lot of marine life will also perish!

Well, why is this happening?

Well, coral reefs mainly die from something called Coral Bleaching. This is when the coral are exposed to hot temperatures or in hot waters, causing the coral to become a white coral (Coral Bleaching)

It's not just coral reef's that are affected by this, it's the whole ocean! humans have been throwing out a lot of trash. Humans aren't trying to throw trash in the ocean but some cargo ships and trash cans can leak plastic, trash, and other waste into the ocean causeing tons of trash to gather up along in swirling areas. The Trash in the ocean is getting worse as the human population increases and the lack of people who are not doing anything. Almost 8,000,000 tons of plastic are leaked from trash cans into the ocean every year, that's a lot.

Animals are also affected by this too. Lots of plastic or trash can get stuck around a sea animals neck
